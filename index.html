    Frequently Asked Questions

    What subjects and grade levels do you tutor?

    NEXTSTEP LEARNING offers online tutoring for students in Grades 1–12. We provide support in Math, Science, English, History & Social Studies and Geography.

    Are sessions online?

    Yes, all tutoring sessions are fully online.

    How long are tutoring sessions, and how often should students meet with a tutor?

    Each tutoring session is 1 hour long. Students can schedule sessions as often as needed, whether they require occasional assistance or ongoing academic support.

    Do you offer group sessions or only one-on-one tutoring?

    Our primary focus is one-on-one tutoring to provide personalized instruction. Group sessions may also be available upon request.

    How do you assess a student's needs before starting?

    Before tutoring begins, we provide a short intake form for parents and students to complete. This helps us understand the student's academic goals, strengths, challenges and learning style so we can tailor sessions to their needs.

    How do I book a tutoring session?

    Booking is easy. Simply contact us through WhatsApp at (437) 545-6524, and we will help schedule a session that works for you.

    What is your cancellation policy?

    We kindly ask that cancellations or rescheduling requests be made at least 24 hours in advance. This allows us to accommodate other students and maintain scheduling flexibility.

    What payment methods do you accept?

    We currently accept payment through Interac e-Transfer, providing a convenient payment process.
